Provide Job Search And Career Coaching For International Students And Immigrants











About:

  • Are you tired of applying to hundreds of jobs online without any results? 
  • Do you know that one of the best methods to learn about great job opportunities and to get interview calls is by networking? 
  • Do you understand the value of your references?





Job hunting in a new country can be hard if you do not understand the basics. It can be even harder if you do not have any professional experience in the new country, with different customs, rules, and nuances. We understand. We’ve been there.



We offer customized job search strategy and career change coaching for international students and immigrants. As previous international students and multiple career-switchers with decades of experience in different continents including North America, Europe, and Asia, you can be confident that your coaches have the experience, skills, and education to support your career growth.



Contact us for creating, developing and reviewing your resume, LinkedIn profile, cover letter; improving your interviewing skills and for other career services.  



Learn more about your coach, Co-founder and International Student Specialist on LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/rameshjayavaram/
